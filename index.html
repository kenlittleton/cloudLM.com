cloudLM - float your licenses on our cloud...

cloudLM is a global, secure, cloud-based software license management service and distribution network for professional media creators in the entertainment, architectual, and product development industries. We provide cloud-based servers for licensing production software products from Adobe, Autodesk, The Foundry, Chaos Group and many other vendors which use FlexLM and RLM floating licenses. We also provide Independent Software Vendors and their customers with on-demand, subscription, and short-term licensing capabilities.

We'll be launching our services soon. Email info@cloudLM.com for more info...

For Individuals

cloudLM is ideal for individuals who would like the flexibility of using floating licenses without the need to setup and maintain their own license server.

For Organizations

As your production moves to the cloud, we provide the security and flexibility to manage and distribute all your licenses easily.

For Vendors

Losing too much revenue to scene releases of your products? cloudLM provides a secure, managed capabilty to license your products. We also provide the capability to offer on-demand, subscription and short-term licensing to furthur increase your revenue.
